# 🎯 HATI Quick Reference Guide

## What is HATI?

**HATI (Human Apex Threat Index)** is your overall power level in Genesis Protocol, inspired by Solo Leveling's hunter ranking system. It combines your stats, mission performance, and special bonuses into a single percentile score (0-100%).

## HATI Formula (Simple)

```
HATI = (Your Stats × 40%) + (Mission XP × 40%) + (Bonuses × 20%)
```
