## How Alignment Modifiers Work

### Lawful Bonus Calculation
```
Alignment Score: -60 (Lawful)
Lawful Bonus = max(0, -(-60)) / 10
             = 60 / 10
             = 6.0 (on 0-10 scale)

Per-Substat Bonuses:
Willpower:  6.0 × 10 = +80
Focus:      6.0 × 10 = +80
Composure:  6.0 × 5  = +25
```

### Neutral Morality (Good-Evil = 0)
```
Good Bonus  = max(0, -0) / 10 = 0
Evil Bonus  = max(0, 0) / 10 = 0
Neutral Bonus = (|0| < 33) ? 5 : 0 = +5

Per-Substat Effects:
Faith:      0×10 - 0×10 = +0
Purpose:    0×5 + 5 = +5
Empathy:    0×8 - 0×5 = +0
Conviction: 5 = +5
```
