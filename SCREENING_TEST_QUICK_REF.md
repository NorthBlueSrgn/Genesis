# 🎮 Quick Reference: Screening Test & Stats

## For New Users

### Option 1: Take the Test ⚡
**What happens:**
- Complete ~15 calibration steps
- Stats calculated from your performance
- Personalized Classified Dossier
- Unique talent profile

**Time:** ~15-20 minutes  
**Result:** Custom stats based on your abilities

---

### Option 2: Skip the Test 🚀
**What happens:**
- Click "Skip" → "Skip All Remaining"
- Receive predetermined Abas profile
- Ready-to-use stats (B-rank overall)
- Standard Classified Dossier

**Time:** ~30 seconds  
**Result:** Solid baseline stats to start immediately

## FAQ

**Q: Can I retake the test later?**  
A: Not currently. You'd need to create a new account.

**Q: Are predetermined stats worse than test-based stats?**  
A: No! They're a solid B-rank profile. Many users would get similar or lower stats from tests.

**Q: What if I skip but change my mind?**  
A: You can't undo the skip. Your stats are saved. But you can improve them through gameplay!

**Q: Will skipping affect my game experience?**  
A: No. The predetermined stats are balanced and give you a great starting point.

**Q: How do I know if I should take the test?**  
A: If you're unsure, skip it! You can always experience the game and decide later if you want to start fresh with a new account.
