### Why This 3-Part Approach?

1. **Accessibility** — Part 1 non-chess warm-up includes all players
2. **Domain Expertise** — Part 2 chess properly measures strategic depth
3. **Real-World Grounding** — Part 3 resource scenario applies theory to practice
4. **Contingency Focus** — All 3 parts test "Plan B" thinking (core strategy skill)
5. **Time-Efficient** — 5–7 min respects cognitive load
6. **Fair Scoring** — Separate parts + averaged (0–30 + 0–50 + 0–20 = 0–100)

### Bonus Mechanics

| Bonus | Part | Value | Trigger |
