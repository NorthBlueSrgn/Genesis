# 📱 Genesis Biometrics & Protocols Guide - Quick Card

## What to Know in 60 Seconds

### The Calibration Test (3 Layers)
```
HARDWARE    → Biometrics + Physical Tests → Physical & Vitality %
SOFTWARE    → Logic + Knowledge Tests    → Intelligence & Creativity %
WETWARE     → Psychology + Ethics        → Spirit & Psyche % + Obsession Level
                            ↓
                      HATI Score (0-100%)
                      + Rank (D-SS+)
                      + Talent Class
                      + Obsession Level
```

### What Gets Locked In After Calibration ✅
- Your Rank (starting point)
- Your Talent Class (doesn't increase during gameplay)
- Your Obsession Level (stays the same)
- Your HATI % baseline
- Your Codename
- Your biometrics (age, height, weight baseline)

### What Changes During Gameplay 🔄
- Your Stats (Physical, Vitality, Intelligence, Creativity, Spirit, Psyche)
- Your Rank (CAN climb from D → SS+)
- Your Daily Metrics (steps, sleep, calories, etc.)
- Your Streaks and Resonance

### Key Distinction: Talent ≠ Destiny
- **Talent Class:** Your learning speed at calibration (Laggard → Genius)
- **Obsession Level:** Your consistency/adherence (Lazy → Compulsive)
- **Reality:** Average Talent + Compulsive Obsession > Genius Talent + Lazy Obsession

### Biometrics vs Daily Metrics
| | Biometrics | Daily Metrics |
|---|---|---|
| When | Calibration (once) | Every day |
| What | Age, height, weight | Steps, sleep, calories |
| Purpose | Define starting "power level" | Track protocol adherence |
| Changes | Never | Always |

### Creating & Finding Protocols
1. **Create:** Go to Order Page, chat with Central AI
2. **Central generates:** Protocol with tasks and stat boosts
3. **Added to:** GameState.paths[]
4. **Found at:** Protocols Page (/paths)
5. **Complete:** Tasks earn XP and improve stats

### HATI/TPI Scores
- **50%:** Global mean (D-E Rank)
- **75%:** Above average (C-B Rank)
- **90%:** Elite tier (S Rank)
- **99%:** Rare asset (SS Rank)
- **99.9%:** Singularity (SS+ Rank)
