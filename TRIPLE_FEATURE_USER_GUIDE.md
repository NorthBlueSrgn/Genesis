## 🎯 Feature Guide

### 🛡️ Decay Recovery (Top of Dashboard)

**When does it appear?**
- When you haven't logged activity for a while
- Shows when decay is imminent (< 1 hour away)

**What to do:**
1. See the urgency badge (🔴 CRITICAL / 🟡 MEDIUM / 🔵 LOW)
2. Click any recovery task
3. Complete the 5-10 minute task
4. Earn bonus XP + prevent decay

**Pro Tip:** Do it early! Critical urgency gives +50% bonus XP

---

### 💡 Smart Suggestions (Main Area)

**How it works:**
- Analyzes your activity patterns
- Finds your weakest stats
- Suggests tasks that help YOU most
- Shows confidence scores (higher = better fit)

**What to do:**
1. Look at top 5 suggestions
2. Read the reason ("Addresses weak stat", "Your peak time")
3. Click a suggestion to start
4. Complete it for maximum value

**Pro Tip:** Click high-confidence tasks first

---

### 📊 Analytics Dashboard (Right Column)

**4 Analytics Cards:**

**1. Metrics & Trends**
- See how efficient you are (XP/min)
- Check task completion rate
- View 7-day trends for each stat

**2. Predictions & ETA**
- Know days until next rank
- See estimated rank date
- Understand confidence level

**3. Focus Areas**
- Find your weakest substats
- See improvement potential
- Get recommendations

**4. Progress Report**
- Read personalized insights
- Understand momentum
- Get next steps

## 🎮 Gameplay Tips

### Maximize Recovery Bonus
```
Recovery Bonus Formula:
- Critical urgency (< 30 min):    +50%
- Medium urgency (< 60 min):      +25%
- 3+ tasks completed:              +30%
- 2 tasks completed:               +15%
- Completed in < 15 min total:    +20%

BEST: Do 3 tasks in critical urgency = +50% + +30% = +80% BONUS!
```

### Maximize Suggestion Value
```
Smart Suggestions prioritize:
1. Tasks fixing your weakest stats (+30 priority)
2. Tasks during your best time (+20 priority)
3. Quick wins when you're busy (+15 priority)
4. Tasks that save your streak (+10 priority)

BEST: Complete high-confidence weak stat tasks!
```

### Maximize Analytics Insight
```
Key Metrics to Watch:
- XP/min: Efficiency score (higher = better)
- Task rate: Hit 70%+ completion
- Days to rank: Track your progression
- Streak multiplier: 10-day streak = 1.5x XP!

BEST: Maintain streak + follow suggestions = Faster rank up
```

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Why isn't recovery widget showing?
**A:** It only shows when decay is imminent (< 1 hour). Log activity to reset it!

### Q: Can I choose any recovery task?
**A:** Yes! Pick any task that fits your time. But urgent tasks give more bonus.

### Q: How accurate are the predictions?
**A:** Confidence % tells you how sure the prediction is. Higher streak = higher confidence.

### Q: What's the fastest way to level up?
**A:** Follow smart suggestions + maintain your streak + watch analytics trends!

### Q: Can I share my analytics?
**A:** Not yet, but it's coming in Phase 2! 🚀
